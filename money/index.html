MY NARRATIVE

Financial education is the key to unlocking doors.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Finance and have spent years in corporate risk analysis. But my real passion for money came from applying these financial models to my own life—a path that allowed me to reach Coast FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) in my early thirties.

Reaching Coast FIRE means I've saved enough that my compound interest will entirely fund my retirement, freeing me up to work on my own terms, consult with amazing clients, and design a stress-free lifestyle.

I truly believe financial education is the ultimate key to unlocking options. When you understand your cash flow and investments, you buy back your agency. My goal is to empower you to live a rich life based entirely around what MATTERS to you—whether that is travel, family, starting a creative business, or securing peace of mind.

"It's not about wealth accumulation for its own sake. It is about buying back your freedom to spend your days exactly how you choose."
